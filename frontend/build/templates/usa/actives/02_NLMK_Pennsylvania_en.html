NLMK Pennsylvania

Location: Farrell, Pennsylvania

Processing capacity: around 0.7 million tonnes of rolled steel per year


The company produces carbon and alloy steel, including hot-rolled, cold-rolled and full hard (which has not been annealed and is particularly hard). Steel from NLMK Pennsylvania is used in the aerospace industry, in mining, car manufacturing and mechanical engineering, in the railway-production and electrical-power industries, etc.

NLMK Pennsylvania is a customer-oriented company which takes an individual approach to its consumers, including in developing the chemical composition of slabs.

The company is part of NLMK USA. In 2013, the division produced 0.7 million tonnes of crude steel and 1.8 million tonnes of rolled steel.

Output

Hot-rolled steel is used in shipbuilding and construction, and in the production of pipelines and pressure vessels.

Cold-rolled steel is used in the manufacture of coated steel, as well as in the production of body parts for machines and equipment, load-bearing structures, pipes, lighting masts, and agricultural equipment.
