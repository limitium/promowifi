NLMK Indiana

Location: Portage, Indiana

Processing capacity: around 0.7 million tonnes of slabs and around 1.1 million tonnes of rolled steel per year


The company produces crude steel and hot-rolled steel and specializes in the production and sale of hot-rolled coils. It produces its output using EAF technology and rolling mills.

NLMK Indiana occupies an advantageous geographical location next to the Port of Indiana on the bank of Lake Michigan, and has access to water, road and rail transport. This means it can increase demand for its products and deliver them to consumers in good time.

Unlike other companies that offer a standard product line, NLMK Indiana welcomes the opportunity to develop new chemical compositions of slabs in collaboration with its customers.

The company is part of NLMK USA. In 2013, the division produced 0.7 million tonnes of crude steel and 1.8 million tonnes of rolled steel.

Output

NLMK Indiana produces slabs with various chemical compositions. The company uses the bulk of its output of slabs in the production of rolled steel at its own mills.

Hot-rolled steel is used in shipbuilding and construction, and the production of pipelines and pressure vessels.
