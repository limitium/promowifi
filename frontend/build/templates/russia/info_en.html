NLMK Russia

This is the Group’s key business segment comprising its mining, coke-chemical, steel production, and rolling assets.

Over 90% of the Company’s steelmaking assets, including the NLMK Kaluga Mini-Mill launched in 2013, are located in Russia. This segment accounts for about 60% of flat steel products and 100% of long products.

NLMK Russia comprises three business units: NLMK Russia Flat, NLMK Russia Long, and NLMK Russia Raw Materials.
