NSMMZ

Location: Revda, Sverdlovsk Region

Processing results: 2.2 million tonnes in 2013


NSMMZ is the backbone of Group NLMK’s Long Products Division in the Urals. Thanks to its state-of-the-art equipment and the latest technology, the company’s products meet the highest quality standards and are highly competitive.

NSMMZ’s production facilities are located in three cities in Sverdlovsk Region: Revda, Berezovsky and Nizhniye Sergi. Revda is home to the company’s modern EAF steelmaking, with a capacity of two million tonnes a year. Here, scrap metal is melted down and recycled into square billets.

In 2007, NSMMZ in Revda converted from OHF to EAF production. This allows alloy steel to be smelted, a metal with a lower content of non-metal oxides and sulphur to be produced, and the temperature of the steel to be regulated smoothly and accurately.

Billets from NSMMZ are sent for further processing to the Berezovsky and Nizhniye Sergi sites. At Nizhniye Sergi, long rebars are manufactured on a 250 rolling mill. Berezovsky has a 150 wire rod-rolling mill, where coil and wire-rod rebar are produced.

Output

Cross-section: 125x125 mm, length: from 8,000 to 12,000 mm from carbon and low-alloy steel. The billets are sent to rolling mills specializing in the production of rebar and other products mainly for the construction industry.

Grades A400, A500C and AT800, sizes 10–40, coil rebar Nos. 6–16 Rebar is used primarily for reinforcing concrete structures.

Diameter from 5.5 to 22 mm from low-carbon, high-carbon and alloy steel. Main consumers – manufacturers of metalware (screws and nails). Wire rod is used primarily in the construction industry.
