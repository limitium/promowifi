VIZ-Steel

Location: Yekaterinburg, Sverdlovsk Region

Processing capacity: 170,000 tonnes of transformer steel per year


VIZ-Steel is a leading global manufacturer of cold-rolled electrical steel and a major manufacturer of transformer steel in Russia. The company has the capacity to produce over 170,000 tonnes of transformer steel. Around 85% of VIZ-Steel’s production is exported.

The innovative technical solutions developed at VIZ-Steel guarantee that the magnetic properties of anisotropic steel are constantly monitored, thereby ensuring their stability.

In 2007, the company launched a programme to upgrade and develop its production facilities by 2015. The programme will make it possible to produce steel with improved properties and of a higher quality, strengthening VIZ-Steel’s leading position on the Russian market and improving its competitiveness on global markets.

Output

VIZ-Steel is one of the leading manufacturers of electrical steel. Together with Novolipetsk, VIZ-Steel produces around 9% of the world’s transformer steel. Transformer (anisotropic) steel is used in the electrical industry for the manufacture of transformer cores and fixed components for electrical equipment.

Its main consumers are electronics companies in over 50 countries, as well as leading Russian electrical and electronic companies and manufacturers of household electrical and radio appliances.
