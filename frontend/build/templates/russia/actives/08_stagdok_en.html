Stagdok

Location: Lipetsk Region

Processing results: 3.9 million tonnes of limestone in 2013


Stagdok was founded in 1928 and is currently one of the leading Russian producers of fluxing limestone, which is used in steelmaking and the cement industry, the construction industry, and in agriculture. Stagdok mines approximately a quarter of all fluxing limestone in Russia.

Stagdok operates in the Sitov area of the Sokol-Sitov fluxing limestone field. Fluxing limestone is used in steel smelting. The field is located 2.5 km from Lipetsk, the location of NLMK Group’s main production site.

Being located near to well-developed transport infrastructure and within close distance of Novolipetsk keeps production and logistical costs to a minimum.

Supplies from Stagdok and Dolomit fully cover NLMK Group’s requirements for the main types of flux.

Output

Fluxing limestone is used as an addition agent in furnace burdens, in pellets or sinter.

Carbonates are used in the production of both limestone used to manufacture construction lime and limestone used as a fluxing material in steelmaking. The stone is mined in an open pit and includes the use of explosives.
