Vtorchermet NLMK

Location: Yekaterinburg, Russia

Processing results:: 2.2 million tonnes in 2013.


Vtorchermet NLMK is NLMK Long’s raw materials unit. The company was established in 1998 as a holding company to collect and process ferrous scrap metal.

Vtorchermet NLMK is represented in over 33 Russian regions and ferrous scrap is collected and processed at around 230 sites.

NLMK Group’s network of scrap processing businesses meets about 85% of the demand for ferrous scrap at the Group’s Russian steelmaking plants. To deal with the increased demand for scrap (with account for the rise in scrap consumption following the launch of NLMK Kaluga), the Group is continuing to develop its scrap processing network. In 2013, NLMK’s scrap processing site in the city of Podolsk commissioned a shredder, thus expanding NLMK’s ferrous scrap processing capacity and maintaining a high level of self-sufficiency after the launch of NLMK Kaluga.

Output

Scrap is sent to NLMK Group companies which are part of NLMK Long. Vtorchermet NLMK meets 85% of the Group’s demand for scrap metal.

Scrap collected at the sites is monitored for radiation and explosiveness and weighed on high-precision scales. Vtorchermet NLMK is the industry leader for scrap metal-processing infrastructure.
