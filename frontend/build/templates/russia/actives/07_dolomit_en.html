Dolomit

Location: Dankov, Lipetsk Region

Processing capacity: 2.3 million tonnes of dolomite per year


Dankov Ballast Plant (now Dolomit) was founded in 1932 and is now a leader among Russian producers of metallurgical dolomite. It is the only company in the business in the Central Federal District.

NLMK acquired the company in the 1990-s as part of the process to establish a raw materials base. The company produces dolomite, a fluxing material used in steelmaking. Supplies from Dolomit and Stagdok fully cover NLMK Group’s requirements for the main types of flux.

The company operates at the Dankov dolomite field located approximately 90 km from Novolipetsk, NLMK Group’s main production site. Being located close to well-developed transport infrastructure is strategically beneficial to consumers.

Output

Dolomite is added to ore when steel is smelted to lower the melting temperature and make it easier to separate the metal from gangue. Dolomite is used in the manufacture of refractories, and in sinter and BOF processes.
