Usage

Hot-rolled steel is mainly used in the production of steel structures, guardrails, ship hulls, machine casings, road-building machinery components, pressure vessels, and building structures.

Usage

Cold-rolled steel is widely used in the production of body parts for machines and equipment, load-bearing structures, pipes, lighting masts, and agricultural equipment.

Usage

Galvanized steel is used in the production of machine body parts, roofing materials, load-bearing structures in hostile environments, and car parts and components. Pre-painted steel with pigmented coatings is used in construction for the production of roofing and finishing materials, and casings for consumer and commercial appliances (white goods). This type of steel boasts high resistance to atmospheric corrosion, has decorative qualities, and combines strength and plasticity.
