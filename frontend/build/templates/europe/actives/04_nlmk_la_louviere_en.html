NLMK La Louviere

Location: La Louvière, Belgium

Processing capacity: 1.7 million tonnes of hot-rolled steel per year, 0.5 million tonnes of cold-rolled steel per year.


La Louvière has existed for over 160 years. It has access to a well-developed transport network: highways, rail links, and waterways. In recent years, investments by NLMK Group have helped the company to overhaul its facilities, improving the quality of its production and expanding the market for its products.

Some of the company’s production material is sold to consumers of hot-rolled steel. The remainder is cold-rolled at the plant or at cold-rolling and coating mills at NLMK Coating (France).

NLMK La Louvière is part of NLMK Europe Strip. It has a 2% share of the European market for flat steel.
