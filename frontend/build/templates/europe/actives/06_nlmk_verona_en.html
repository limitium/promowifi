NLMK Verona

Location: Verona, Italy

Processing capacity: around 450,000 tonnes of steel products per year


The company was founded in 2004. NLMK Verona specializes in the production of thick plates, slabs and forging ingots used as blanks for the manufacture of mill rollers, thick plate, turbine rotors, etc.

NLMK Verona is a modern steelworks. Thanks to investment and overhauls, including to its continuous casting machine, the company has widened its product range and is able to meet consumer demand for high-quality flat steel up to 1,000 mm thick and slabs up to 400 mm thick.

Around two thirds of goods produced are sold in Italy. Apart from European countries, the company also supplies Asia and the American continent.

The company is part of NLMK Europe Plate. It has an 8% share of the EU market for thick plate.

Output

NLMK Verona produces high-quality steel plate with a thickness of 20 mm to 1,000 mm. It is used in shipbuilding, mechanical engineering and infrastructure construction. Apart from thick plate, the company also produces slabs of up to 400 mm in thickness and forging ingots weighing from 5 to 80 tonnes. NLMK Verona is one of the largest manufacturers of forging ingots in Europe.
