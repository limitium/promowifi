NLMK DanSteel A/S

Location: Frederiksvaerk, Denmark

Processing capacity: 0.7 million tonnes of steel plate per year


NLMK DanSteel A/S was established in 2002 from Danish Steel Works Ltd. In December 2012, NLMK DanSteel completed the revamp of its rolling mill and started production of 4,200 mm thick plate, adding new products to the company’s portfolio. Total investments in the project exceeded EUR 120 million. The overhaul gave DanSteel the opportunity to reinforce its position in the market for thick steel plate production.

The company has an advantageous geographical location with its own port, allowing it to optimize both receipts of raw materials and deliveries of end products to consumers.

Slabs for manufacturing plates at DanSteel are supplied by NLMK Group’s production site in Lipetsk, guaranteeing the company a stable supply of steel of the highest quality.

In May 2014, NLMK DanSteel joined the European programme to improve foundation structures at offshore wind farms. As an experiment under the programme, the company has delivered 200 tonnes of high-duty, thick steel plate produced to special sizes. This will be used to manufacture poles used in the foundations of offshore wind turbines.

Output

NLMK DanSteel produces a wide range of high quality thick plates for manufacturers of wind turbines, drilling rigs, yellow goods, and shipbuilders and mechanical-engineering and infrastructure companies, primarily for the Northern European market.

NLMK DanSteel is a leading supplier of thick plates for North European wind farms and views this segment as the most promising and strategically important, not only in terms of business development, but also as part of NLMK Group's policy aimed at promoting advanced environmental technologies.
