NLMK Strasbourg

Location: Strasbourg, France

Processing capacity: around 0.2 million tonnes of coated steel per year.


The company was founded in 1890. NLMK Strasbourg specializes in the production of galvanized steel and pre-painted steel. As a raw material, the company uses hot-rolled steel supplied by NLMK La Louvière. The Group’s unique business model allows it to monitor all the stages in high-quality steel production, from mining to the manufacture of high value added goods.

NLMK Strasbourg provides customers with high-quality services via its own modern service centre.

The company is part of NLMK Europe Strip. It has a 2% share of the EU market for flat steel.

Output

Galvanized steel is used in the production of load-bearing structures in hostile environments, car parts, machine body parts, etc.

Pre-painted steel is used in construction for the production of roofing and finishing materials, and casings for consumer and commercial appliances. This type of steel boasts high resistance to atmospheric corrosion, has decorative qualities, and combines strength and plasticity.
